Why is Painting Pelicans?

Painting Pelicans emerged from collaborations between our lead vocalist Harry and our guitarist Jonas.

Jonas and Harry started recording music together in early 2021, initially intending to record a Harry solo album. However, we realized we wrote good music together, and felt that expanding the project to a full band made the most sense.

Since then, we've expanded to perform full-band shows, wrote, self-recorded, and self-produced an entire album's worth of songs (out Nov. 17, 2023), and have played to hundreds of people across Ontario.

Thanks for reading! We're absolutely stoked if our music makes you groove (even just a little bit)!

Electronic Press Kit

Painting Pelicans strives to make every show memorable and fun. Our music touches on themes that are universal (love and its ups and downs, uncertainty, and joy). Our lyrics toe the line between deeply personal and generally applicable. Our sound is always evolving, with our goal to hit hard and be catchy.

We have a passion for music and we want to share it with as many people as we possibly can.
